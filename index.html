General Overview

Welcome to the official wiki for the Shinro Shippuden Naruto Minecraft Mod. This wiki contains guides on stats, jutsu mechanics, clan breakdowns, and more to help you in your ninja journey.

Getting started

  • Version - 1.19.2
  • Dependancy mod link (Required)

How to use jutsu?

  • When you spawn into a new shinro world youll be assigned a clan, 1-3 elements, and possibly a kkg or dojutsu
  • You can see the handseals for these jutsu by pressing M than clicking jutsu information and clicking on the button corrosponding to the handseals youre looking for
  • Press the keybinds in the order and at the correct speed as the handseals provided to use a jutsu!
  • Each justu requires you to be a certain rank or higher
  • Read the next info card to learn how to rank up!

Ranking up, points, and leveling

  • To gain xp all you need to do is kill mobs and later down the line complete quests
  • Once you have enough xp you'll level up and get a random amount of points each level up
  • These points can be used on the three main stats, ninjutsu which increases your chakra reserve, medical which increases your health, and taijutsu which opens up new tai skills aswell as buffs their potency
  • After enough leveling you will rank up allowing you to use new jutsu and progress your powers

Moving forward

  • Chakra control - All you need to do to activate this is shift than click your chakra charge button!
  • Weapons - There is a wide variety of weapons in the mod which a craftable from all development stages, crafting them early will help you progress fast!

General jutsu scrolls

  • Theres a chance to recieve one of these jutsu scrolls when leveling up
  • Will give jutsu from C to S rank which every shinobi can use regardless of clan, kkg, etc

DNA

Please note this process is supposed to be hard, its a hard character reset and is end game content and shouldnt be attempted early on.

How to use dna

  • To start create a growth vile using glass panes, a nether star, and a chorus fruit
  • Keep this in your inventory while the new body grows and develops
  • Craft and use a summoning scroll and place it in your left hand than rightclick the fully grown growth vile to spawn the lab grown ninja
  • Find the living corpse reanimation jutsu in general jutsu scrolls (S rank) and use it on the spawned ninja
  • Do use do the last step you must have 500 ninjutsu, 500 medical, and be S rank

Commands

This section will show what the types avalible to plug in are and what the different values do. All set string values and types should be lowercase, if it only shows a type and not a value its because its intuitive.

/setstring username type value

  • kkg
  • lefteye / righteye
  • clan
  • elements - can be multiple values

/setnumber username type value

  • sharinganLevel - 1 to 5 can be entered, 1 to 3 are tomoes, 4 is ms, 5 is ems
  • rank - 0 to 6, f rank being 0, s rank being 6
  • points
  • taijutsu
  • ninjutsu
  • medical
  • sageStage - 0 to 2, 0 and 1 are the training stages, 2 means you have access and can use sage mode freely
  • sageType - 1 to 4, only visual differences
  • msType - 1 to 5, different types of mangekyo
  • beastTimer
  • beastType - 1 to 9
  • beastLevel - 0 to 6

Each ms ability comes from each eye like in the show, so for example if you have itachis ms and only have youre left eye youre only able to use Tsukuyomi and cant use Amaterasu unless you transplant a right eye and vice versa

Susanoo

Sage mode

General

  • Amount of nature energy you can build is 25% of your max chakra
  • If your nature energy meter fills up in and out of training you will turn into stone

Obtainment

Stage 1

  • Must have at least 750 ninjutsu and 500 medical to start training
  • To start locate a sage biome
  • Once found enter the water which in this biome acts as senjutsu oil
  • Balance your chakra in this water by charging and stopping charge of your own chakra while youre filled with nature energy
  • When your nature energy bar is half full theres a chance you master the balance and can move on to stage two

Stage 2

  • Charge senjutsu (New keybind) at 200 or above on the y level
  • As long as youre charging it without going over your limit theres a 1/500 chance every second to become a full sage (Roughly 8 minutes of training nothing too crazy)

Sage powers

  • The nature energy youve built up will act as a battery for your standard chakra, whenever you run out it will refill it at its own cost
  • Highest strength bonus in the mod
  • Along with strength get haste, resistance, speed, and NV

Curse mark

Obtainment

  • Trade with a expert level cleric villager to get snake sage blood
  • Craft the ritual using the sage blood
  • Use it on yourself or others to start the process
  • Only a 10% chance of survival
  • 5 forms you can get, all have the same benefits just different visually, all 5 have unique first and second forms
  • If you want a new form you can keep using rituals and if its successful youll get a new type

Stages

Stage one

Can be activated via keybind press and grants minor strength and speed but mainly gives chakra regen

Stage two
  • Activated when someone in stage one is attacked and under 25% hp
  • Flight, high strength, higher speed, chakra regen
  • Turning off the cm after reaching stage two will temporarily stun you

Mark aching

  • Every once in a while once you get your cm your mark will ache and youll experience some adverse effects
  • After being in your curse mark form for long enough you will gain control over this and it wont happen again

Other

  • Before gaining control of your curse mark you can only use it for 5 minutes, after gaining control youre able to maintain it for 10
  • After turning off your curse mark youll have to wait 5 minutes before using it again

  • Lower the tailed beast to 25% or lower hp

  • Rightclick the scroll with 700 medical or more to implant it

  • Uzumaki reaper death seal
  • Senju wood bind
  • Lava ash seal
  • Killing ice spears
  • Coercion (must have 3 tomoes)
  • Rinnegan soul steal

Stages

  • Partial eye transformation
  • One tailed chakra cloak
  • Two tailed gore cloak
  • Four tails bone cloak
  • Partial transformation (A beast player mix visually, different with each beast)
  • Full transformation (Become a smaller version of whichever tailed beast you contain)
